About us

Who we are

A specialised law firm focused on tax, criminal and commercial law

CLAY & PARTNERS is not another full-service law firm that claims to do everything. We believe that the right approach is genuine specialisation — so that every client receives a service of the highest quality. Our work is grounded in the long-standing expertise of our lawyers in their chosen fields, which enables us to deliver what a client rightfully expects from a qualified legal adviser. We know what we are good at, and that is what we do. We are strong advocates of a practical and pragmatic approach to the law. We do not burden you with statutory provisions — we solve your problems. Effectively.

Why choose us
We don't back down
"A ship is safe in harbour, but that's not what ships are for." (William G.T. Shedd)

Fear does not paralyse us — it drives us forward. People often stagnate in the face of concerns that have been blown out of proportion: reluctant to dispute a tax authority decision, to stand up to a multinational, or to assert rights they are legally entitled to, all because of the risk of failure. That is not how we think. We will not allow fear to consume our clients, because we know that where there is justice, there is a legal remedy.

Success is a commitment

Winning has become second nature to us. We know that an advocate's greatest credential is the victories achieved alongside their clients. Success propels us forward — it is our daily motivation and the reason we practise law. We also recognise that maintaining the highest quality of service is an exceptionally demanding task, which is why we approach every matter with full commitment and drive. Because success is a commitment.

We deliver efficiently

An outstanding team and first-rate legal services are a given — but we know that an equally essential part of success is correct timing in terms of efficiency and case management. As the saying goes, we know how to "eat the elephant one bite at a time": even the most sprawling legal problem, a protracted dispute or an apparently insoluble case can be taken on, structured, risk-assessed, cost-optimised and phased appropriately — with our fee structure designed to match. We do not burden clients with legal complexity; we deliver effective solutions.

Our client's success is our priority

Exemplary client care is a matter of course. Positive feedback and the overall satisfaction of our clients are the cornerstone of our practice. To deliver a truly elite legal service, we consider it essential to treat every client individually and with the legal empathy their situation demands — whether that means resolving a contentious dispute, optimising a tax structure, establishing a sound corporate framework or securing the outcome our client deserves.

Practice areas

We provide legal representation and advisory services in key areas of tax, commercial and dispute resolution law. Our work focuses on matters where we have long-term experience and deep specialist expertise.

Are you in dispute with the tax or customs authority? Unsure how to navigate a tax audit? Is your export business facing an unjustified withholding of VAT refunds? Has a freezing order been imposed on your company? Representing domestic and foreign clients at every stage of tax proceedings — including any subsequent court challenge — is our core practice. We are fully aware of how protracted tax litigation can be, yet we are firmly convinced that mounting a proper defence is always worthwhile. We have an established track record of successfully resolving disputes with the tax administration with a combined value exceeding CZK 1 billion.

Has your company suffered loss at the hands of a public authority? Have you been deprived of funds as a result of an unlawful intervention by the tax office? Were you subjected to unlawful pre-trial detention? We represent clients in claims against the state under Act No. 82/1998 Coll. on liability for damage caused in the exercise of public authority by an unlawful decision or improper official conduct. We will pursue your claim for compensation and secure appropriate redress. Do not hesitate to challenge unlawful state conduct. It pays to fight back.

We go to court. Frequently. And we enjoy it. We have successfully represented clients in, among other things, claims for unpaid insurance proceeds following natural disasters, disputes over the validity of employment contracts, defect liability claims and a wide range of other predominantly commercial disputes. In insolvency proceedings, we also act for insolvency administrators and creditors in adversary proceedings. Business is not always straightforward — it brings with it a host of problems that we help our clients resolve. And when agreement cannot be reached, we fight our clients' corner in court with full force.

Are you selling or acquiring a company? Concerned as a seller about potential liability attaching to your interest, or do you need, as a buyer, to negotiate appropriate warranties in respect of the shares being acquired? Troubled by problematic shareholders or partners? Our firm protects clients' rights across a wide spectrum of corporate matters — simply ask. We also act for creditors and insolvency administrators in insolvency proceedings.

Drawing on our expertise in tax law, we also defend clients in criminal proceedings, with a particular focus on economic crime, tax offences and white-collar crime. We have successfully implemented compliance programmes at a number of corporations, designed to prevent the commission of criminal offences with the primary aim of protecting company management under the Corporate Criminal Liability Act. JUDr. Jakub Hlína is a member of the Union of Defence Counsels of the Czech Republic.

We also advise on selected matters of public law regulation, particularly in the areas of railway law, aviation law and subsidy law. We represent our clients in a wide range of administrative proceedings, including price control reviews and subsidy compliance audits.

An employment dispute does not necessarily mean a dispute before a court — we endeavour to minimise conflict in the workplace between employees and employers before it escalates. We are equally experienced in identifying and addressing unlawful or unfair practices by employers against employees, and we offer legal assistance in disputes between employers and employees over claims arising from the employment relationship.

We handle personal and family matters of a sensitive nature. A typical example is the settlement of jointly held matrimonial property — because obtaining a divorce is something most people can manage, but settling what comes after is an altogether different matter.

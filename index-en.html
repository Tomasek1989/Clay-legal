About us

Who we are

A specialised firm focused on tax and commercial law

CLAY & PARTNERS is a law firm with a narrow focus on tax and commercial law. Our team members have extensive experience in both advisory and contentious matters and are accustomed to working in environments where timing and precision of every legal step matter. We do not provide general advice — we find specific solutions to each client's situation.

Our values
Specialisation

We do not try to cover all areas of law. We focus on areas where we have genuine depth of knowledge and experience.

Pragmatic approach

We see law as a tool for solving real problems. The outcome for the client is more important to us than legal theory.

Efficiency

Correct timing and precision of the legal approach determine the outcome. Unnecessary delays are always detrimental to the client.

Client success

The results our clients have achieved with our help are our greatest reference. We handle every case individually.

Practice areas

We provide legal representation and advice in key areas of tax, commercial and contentious law. We focus on matters in which we have long-term experience and deep specialist knowledge.

We represent clients in disputes with the tax administration in tax proceedings and before administrative courts.

Are you dealing with the tax or customs authority? Facing a tax audit, withholding of VAT refunds or security orders? We represent domestic and foreign clients at all stages of tax proceedings, including subsequent judicial review. We have a track record of successful disputes with the tax administration exceeding CZK 1 billion in value.

We represent clients in claims against the state for unlawful conduct by public authorities.

If the state has caused you harm through an unlawful decision or improper official procedure, we will help you seek compensation under the State Liability Act. We represent clients in cases of unlawful intervention by the tax administration and other public authorities.

We represent clients in court proceedings primarily in the field of commercial and civil law.

We regularly represent clients in proceedings relating to commercial disputes, liability for defects, insurance claims and employment law claims. Where settlement is not achievable, we defend our clients' interests before the courts with full commitment.

We provide legal advice in business operations, transactions and shareholder disputes.

We assist clients with the sale and acquisition of companies, structuring relationships between shareholders and resolving shareholder disputes. We also represent creditors and insolvency administrators in insolvency proceedings.

We defend clients in criminal proceedings, primarily in the area of economic crime.

We specialise in criminal proceedings relating to economic and tax offences — so-called white-collar crimes. We also assist clients in establishing preventive measures to avoid corporate criminal liability. JUDr. Jakub Hlína is a member of the Union of Defence Counsels of the Czech Republic.

We address selected issues of public law regulation and represent clients before administrative bodies.

We represent clients in proceedings before administrative bodies, for example in price controls or audits of subsidy use. We also focus on issues relating to regulated industries.

We assist both employers and employees in resolving disputes arising from employment.

We strive to resolve disputes between employers and employees efficiently and to prevent escalation. Where court proceedings are unavoidable, we represent clients in employment law disputes concerning claims arising from the employment relationship.

We represent clients in disputes over the settlement of matrimonial property.

Family disputes require a sensitive and individual approach. We help clients resolve property matters connected with divorce, particularly the settlement of jointly owned matrimonial property.
